Analyze Blood Flow View

This is the blood flow visualization of our tool. Brain and dendrogram views encode the flow of blood in the arteries. Saturation from red to orange shows the volume of blood in vessel. There are two view : Brain and Dendrogram. Dendrograms represent branching in brain arteries in an abstract way. Arteries where stroke is detected is highlighted in grey color.

There are 3 views to look at 3D brain projection

  1. Back
  2. Top
  3. Lateral

We support two tree diagrams which can be switched between using the buttons on dashboard :

  1. Arc trees : They represent the branching but we don't encode the oringal length of arteries
  2. Rectangular trees : Here we also represent the length of the arteries in addition to branching.

Analyze Symmetry

This is the symmetry view of our tool. Brain and dendrogram views the part of the brain arteries belong to. Categorical color code is used to highlight branches which supply blood to different parts of the brain. There are two view : Brain and Dendrogram.
